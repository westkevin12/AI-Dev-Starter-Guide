# Contributing to AI-Dev-Starter-Guide

Thank you for your interest in improving this guide! We welcome contributions from the community.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork** the repository.
2.  **Clone** your fork locally.
3.  **Create a branch** for your feature or fix (`git checkout -b feature/amazing-improvement`).
4.  **Make changes**.
5.  **Test** your changes (especially if modifying `install.sh`).
6.  **Commit** your changes (`git commit -m "Add amazing improvement"`).
7.  **Push** to your fork (`git push origin feature/amazing-improvement`).
8.  Open a **Pull Request**.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a typo, a broken link, or a bug in the installation script, please [open an issue](https://github.com/westkevin12/AI-Dev-Starter-Guide/issues) describing the problem and how to reproduce it.
